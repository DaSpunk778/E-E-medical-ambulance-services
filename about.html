Our Story

Born from a
real need.

We started E&E Medical because we saw a gap. In moments of emergency, people were struggling to find a reliable ambulance fast enough. Calls went unanswered. Vehicles were unavailable. Time — the most critical resource in any emergency — was being wasted.

So we built a solution. A coordination service that connects people in need to verified ambulance providers instantly — cutting through the delays and giving every second the value it deserves.

Today, E&E Medical handles emergency response, hospital transfers and event medical standby across Lagos. And we're just getting started.

We respond because every second counts. When a call comes in, we move immediately — no hold music, no waiting. Just fast, coordinated dispatch. Real coordination. Real speed.
